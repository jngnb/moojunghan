# On Writing Things Down

There's something about committing thoughts to text that forces clarity.

When it's just in your head, ideas can be vague and half-formed. The act of writing — of choosing specific words — is itself a kind of thinking.

I've been trying to write more. Not for anyone else, just for myself. A record of what I was thinking at a given time.
